About the M.S. by Research Degree

Officially Equivalent to M.Tech

The M.S. by Research degree is formally recognized by AICTE as equivalent to M.E./M.Tech. degrees (Page-22, Part-III, Sec-4, Sl. No.-9 at AICTE documentation).

Program Structure:

  • 2-3 year rigorous program selected via GATE scores and interviews
  • Combines advanced coursework with significant research thesis work
  • Minimum CGPA requirement of 7.5 with publications in reputed conferences/journals
  • Enhanced focus on research and development capabilities
